FAQs

How do I submit a commission request?

You can submit a commission by tapping the "+" button in the Fill Out Form, then fill out the commission form and provide the necessary details and references before tapping the submit button.

How will I know if my commission is accepted?

You can check the Commission tab from time to time to see whether the artist has reviewed or followed up on your request.

Can I edit my commission request after submission?

Yes, after pending requests may still be edited before they are accepted by the artist.

How long does it take to complete a commission?

Completion time depends on the complexity of the artwork and the current commission queue.

Can I cancel my commission request?

The artist reserves the right to accept or decline any request. Clients may cancel pending requests before acceptance through communications. Accepted commissions may only be canceled under agreed conditions.
