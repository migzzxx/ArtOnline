Privacy Policy

ArtOnline respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when filling out commission forms, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Contact number
  • Art preferences and details
  • Uploaded reference images

2. How We Use Your Information:

Your information is used solely for:

  • Processing commission requests
  • Communication between client and artist
  • Managing commission status and updates

3. Data Storage

All submitted information is securely stored in the system database and is accessible only to the artist and authorized system users.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is only used within the scope of the commission process.

5. File Uploads

Any images or files you upload are used strictly as references for your commission and are not distributed publicly without consent.

6. Data Retention

Your data will be retained as long as necessary for commission processing and record-keeping purposes.

7. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access.

8. User Rights

You may request access, modification, or deletion of your personal data by contacting the artist.

9. Third-Party Services

This website does not rely on third-party advertising or data-selling services.

10. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the service means acceptance of changes.
